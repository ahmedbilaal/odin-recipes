Burger

Description

A hamburger, or burger, is a flat round cake, typically containing meat, vegetables, and sometimes other ingredients, with toppings placed between a bun or on a biscuit. The burger is commonly served with ketchup, mustard, relish, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Vegetable
  • Bun

Steps

  1. Preheat
  2. Drop the meat
  3. Flip and cheese.
  4. Ready the bun
  5. Assemble burger
